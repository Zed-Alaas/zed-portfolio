01 — about

Curiosity,
compiled.

I'm Zed — a web developer who believes the web should feel alive. I care about the details most people scroll past: the easing curve of a hover state, the 40 ms that separate "fast" from "instant", the layout that works just as well on a cracked phone screen as on a 4K monitor.

I build with modern JavaScript, semantic HTML and CSS that doesn't fight back. Lately I've been deep in animation and interaction design — making interfaces that respond, react and reward exploration.

When I'm not building for the browser, I'm building for wrists — Summit, my Apple Watch climbing tracker — or out with a camera chasing light.
